Privacy Policy.

Effective Date: 1 January 2026  ·  Last Updated: 1 April 2026

Overview

Irrigation Hub Studio ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of farmers and administrators who use our platform. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Short version: We collect only what we need to run the irrigation platform. We do not sell your data. You can delete your account at any time.

Data We Collect

Data TypeWhat We CollectWhy
Account Info Name, email, hashed password, role Account creation and authentication
Farm Fields Field name, location, area, GPS coordinates Weather integration and irrigation scheduling
Sensor Data Soil moisture, temperature, humidity Real-time field monitoring
Irrigation Logs Amount applied, optimal amount, date/time Usage analytics and efficiency reports
Notifications Push subscription tokens, email address Irrigation alerts and system notifications
Usage Metadata Login timestamps, page views Security and platform improvement

How We Use Your Data

  • Providing the Service — Running irrigation schedules, generating water usage charts, and sending alerts
  • Weather Integration — Your field's GPS coordinates are sent to third-party weather APIs (OpenWeatherMap) to retrieve local conditions
  • Platform Analytics — Anonymised, aggregated data is used to compute "platform average" comparisons shown in the analytics dashboard
  • Security — Login records help us detect and prevent unauthorised access
  • Communication — Irrigation due alerts, system updates, and account-related emails

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on MongoDB Atlas (cloud-hosted, encrypted at rest). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. API routes are protected with JWT tokens that expire automatically.

We implement industry-standard security practices including HTTPS, environment-variable secret management, and role-based access control (Farmer vs Admin).

Third-Party Services

  • OpenWeatherMap — Weather data for your field coordinates
  • MongoDB Atlas — Cloud database hosting (AWS infrastructure)
  • Web Push (VAPID) — Browser push notifications
  • Nodemailer / SMTP — Email delivery for irrigation alerts

We do not integrate advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms that track you across websites.

Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update your name, email, and location via the Edit Profile page
  • Deletion — Request full account and data deletion via the Contact page
  • Portability — Request your irrigation logs in CSV format
  • Objection — Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

Cookies

We use localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store your authentication token and user preferences on your device. No third-party tracking cookies are used. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.
